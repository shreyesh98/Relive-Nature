WHAT WE DO

We Are Trying To Save The Planet Earth

This website is shaped by a team of passionate individuals who believe in green living and see the world with the eyes of ancestors who enjoyed their living in true, beautiful nature. The world was an amazing place then with full of stories, beauty and natural wonder. We want to inspire people through media, personal experience and digital platform to explore and protect the diverse and beautiful natural world. In existing world you have to escape from the hustle and bustle of city life for the opportunity to experience the natural world. It is the time for awareness and education about the natural environment to people of all ages and to protect and enhance their surroundings like an Arboretum as a heaven of sanctuary for native plants and animals. Our mission is to protect and conserve wildlife and their habitats here in India by engaging people and advocating on behalf of nature. It's high time to safeguard nature's critical services for well-being of us as well as future generations.

Mission

Protecting nature, for people today and future generations, the mission of Relive Nature is to Educate conserve the lands, waters and the airborne on which all life depends.

Vision
